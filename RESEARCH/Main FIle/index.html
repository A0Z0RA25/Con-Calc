Circle

Definition

  • is formed when a plane cuts the cone at right angels to its axis.
  • A circle is the locus of all points in the plane having the same fixed positive distance ,called the radius from a fixed point, called the center.

    • Parts Of Circle

    • Radius - the distance from the center.
    • Circumference - the distance around the circle.

    STANDARD FORM OF CIRCLE with center(h,k):

    (x - h)²+(y - K)² = r²

    Example: General - standard form

    Given: x²-14x+y²+2y+14=0

    Solution:

    x²-14x+y²+2y=-14 - Combine similar terms and transpose 14.

    (x²-14x+49)+(y²+2y+1)=-14+49+1 - Completing square.

    (x-7)²+(y+1)²=36 - Factor (x²-14x+49) and (y²+2y+1)

    C:(7,-1); r=6 - By inspection, h=7, k=-1, and r=6

    GENERAL FORM OF CIRCLE

    Ax²+Ay²+Cx+Dy+E=0

    Example: Standard - General form

    Given: (x+1)²+(y-2)²=3

    Solution:

    x²+2x+1+(y²-4y+4)-3=0 - Apply square of binomial and APE.

    x²+y²+2x-4y+2=0 - Combine like terms and follow the format of general form.
